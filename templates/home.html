Know the air you breathe.

At LibAirty, we've proven that outdoor weather has a significant effect on the quality of air we breathe. We know what weather trends to look for, and we can use that knowledge to predict the kind of air will be brought into your home.

The gauge in the section below displays what we predict the air quality will be in your room in the next hour if you leave your windows open. If the air pollution levels are predicted to enter the red-zone (500+ PPM), you will be prompted to close your windows. This way, we're preventing some seriously polluted air from entering your room.

The next time we forecast the pollution levels in your room to drop below the red-zone, we'll trigger a prompt to open your windows and get the air circulating again. And this time, we don't have to worry about any nasty pollutants entering our air and pushing us back into the red-zone... at least not for the next hour!
